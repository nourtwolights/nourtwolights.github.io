001/005

Expo 2020 Dubai(SAP Customer Checkout)

This project involved supporting and customizing the SAP Customer Checkout product for Expo 2020 Dubai. The primary goal was to enhance the retail and F&B systems across various pavilions at the expo.

002/005

HCMS PERFORM

HCMS PERFORM is an advanced HR management system designed to streamline and optimize various HR processes, facilitating efficient coordination and management of human resources across different countries, including Egypt and KSA. The system encompasses a wide range of functionalities to support comprehensive HR operations.

003/005

E-invoice Zatca

The E-invoice Implementation project for the KSA Zakat Government aimed to ensure compliance with local e-invoicing regulations by integrating electronic invoicing functionalities into the SAP Customer Checkout (CCO) system. This project focused on generating and printing QR codes for Simplified Tax Invoices using TLV encoding, a key requirement for e-invoicing compliance in Saudi Arabia.

004/005

Egypt Traffic System

The Egypt Traffic System project aimed to develop a comprehensive, centralized traffic management solution to streamline and automate various traffic-related services. The system was designed to facilitate data entry, processing, and reporting across all traffic units in the government traffic system.

005/005

Al-Habib Real Estate

This project involved the integration and customization of the Oracle Cloud ERP (NetSuite) for Al-Habib Real Estate, focusing on enhancing financial management processes and improving system functionality to meet the client's specific needs.
